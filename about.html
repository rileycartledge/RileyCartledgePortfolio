Course Overview

Welcome to my Creative Digital Media portfolio! This semester our class has been exploring a variety of techniques and tools to create some pretty cool digital content. We've used Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Animate, and Adobe Dreamweaver as our main tools.

Here's a quick rundown of skills we've learnt:

  • Creating monochromatic landscapes
  • Removing backgrounds
  • Designing alphabet art and images within text
  • Developing cartoon characters
  • Mastering selection and masking techniques
  • Producing mirror effects
  • Designing postcards
  • Coloring sketches
  • Applying transparent effects
  • Blending multiple images into a single creation
  • Understanding animation fundamentals and principles
  • Creating frame-by-frame animations
  • Adding sound to our animations
  • Utilizing motion, classic, and shape tweens
  • Learning the basics of HTML for web design

These projects have been a lot of fun and have allowed us to experiment and grow our skills in digital media.
